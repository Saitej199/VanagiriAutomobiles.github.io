Vision

Design

The Vanagiri GT-e Concept features a sleek and aerodynamic design, inspired by the dynamic curves of Indian architecture and the fluidity of different traditional forms. The body will be crafted from lightweight materials, ensuring optimal performance and agility on the track.

Performance

Powered by a cutting-edge hybrid powertrain, the Vanagiri GT-e Concept delivers exhilarating performance while maintaining eco-friendliness. The advanced technology ensures seamless power delivery, making every drive a thrilling experience.

Representation

As the first sports car concept representing India on the global stage, the Vanagiri GT-e Concept aims to redefine the perception of Indian automotive engineering. It embodies the spirit of progress and excellence that defines the nation's automotive industry. The Vanagiri GT-E Concept is more than just a sports car; it's a symbol of India's ambition and vision for the future. With its striking design, exceptional performance, and innovative technology, it's set to make a mark on the global sports car market and showcase India's potential on the world stage.
